My Places I Visited On My Trip

Washington D.C and Virginia Beach

Once I went on a school trip to Virginia Beach and Washington D.C.
We went on this trip to visit different colleges.
We stayed at this hotel in Virginia Beach that was on the bay.
There was a sign there that said"Do Not Swim In The Bay".
Well one of my classmates decided to get in the bay. Everyone was telling him not to do it but he did it anyway.
Well, while he was in the bay he ended up getting stung by a jellyfish.
It wasn't funny at first but it became funny because he wouldn't listen to anyone. That's why it's important to follow the rules.

We also visited Washington D.C.. We seen the White House and we were not allowed to enter but we got to stand in front of it.
It was very nice and cool.

Another place that we visited was the American Art Museum. It was a beautiful place and a lot of famous people have visited it.
I really enjoyed the museum and I had a great time.
